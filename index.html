About the Practice

Experienced, Compassionate Psychiatric Care

I'm Lisa Anderson, MSN, PMHNP-BC — a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and founder of Anderson Psychiatric Care. As a solo provider, I personally evaluate and treat every patient, ensuring continuity, consistency, and a truly individualized approach to care.

You can expect thoughtful, personalized treatment with regular check-ins — especially when starting or adjusting medications — to ensure your care is both effective and well-tolerated. Clear communication and timely follow-up are central to my practice and to how I support each patient.

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Indiana University of Pennsylvania, my Master of Science in Nursing Administration from La Roche College in Pittsburgh, and completed my Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner training at Regis College in Weston, Massachusetts. With more than 35 years of nursing experience — including a decade in public health school nursing, as well as inpatient psychiatric and community healthcare settings — I bring a broad, well-rounded perspective to psychiatric care.

My focus is on reducing symptoms, strengthening coping skills, and helping you move toward greater stability, resilience, and overall well-being.

Services

Services Offered

Below are the core services I offer — all delivered via secure telehealth, so you can meet with me from the comfort of your own home.

Initial Evaluation

Determine whether medication is clinically indicated. Some patients may be referred out for therapy.

Medication Management

We'll talk through your medication options, monitor how things are going, adjust doses when needed, and walk through what to expect at every step.

Symptom Assessment

A careful review of how severe your symptoms are, how long they've been with you, and the patterns I'm seeing so we land on the right level of care.

Collaboration

Assistance with connecting you to a therapist.

Telehealth Visits

All of our appointments happen over secure video — convenient, private, and accessible from anywhere in Massachusetts or Rhode Island.

Patient Education

I want you to leave each visit understanding your diagnosis, how your medications work, what to watch for, and when we should follow up.
