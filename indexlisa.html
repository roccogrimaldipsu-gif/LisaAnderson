About the Practice

Experienced, Compassionate Psychiatric Care

Coastal Psychiatric Services is the independent practice of Lisa Anderson, MSN, PMHNP-BC. As the sole practitioner, Lisa personally evaluates and treats every patient. There are no rotating providers, no rushed visits, and no handoffs. The care you receive at your first appointment is the same care you receive at every appointment.

Lisa earned her Bachelor's degree in Nursing from Indiana University of Pennsylvania, followed by a Master's in Nursing Administration from LaRoche College in Pittsburgh, PA. She then completed an additional Master's program earning her license as a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP) at Regis College in Weston, MA.

With over 35 years of extensive nursing experience (including a decade in public health school nursing), Lisa brings a uniquely broad perspective to psychiatric care. Her background spans inpatient psychiatric hospitals, adolescent and adult inpatient facilities, and community healthcare settings. The practice prioritizes alleviating symptoms, strengthening coping mechanisms, and guiding individuals toward stability and fulfillment.

Services

What We Offer

Coastal Psychiatric Services provides thorough psychiatric evaluations and personalized medication management, entirely via telehealth, from the comfort of your home.

Initial Evaluation

A comprehensive assessment of your mental health history, symptoms, medical background, and previous treatments to form a clear clinical picture.

Medication Management

Discussion of medication options, ongoing monitoring, dosage adjustments, and education about what to expect throughout your treatment journey.

Symptom Assessment

Detailed review of symptom severity, duration, and patterns to determine the appropriate level of care and best-fit treatment approach.

Collaborative Planning

You and your provider work together to create a personalized treatment plan, which may include medication, lifestyle changes, and coordinated therapy referrals.

Telehealth Visits

All appointments are conducted via secure video. Convenient, private, and accessible from anywhere in Massachusetts and Rhode Island.

Patient Education

We ensure you understand your diagnosis, how medications work, what side effects to watch for, and when follow-up is needed.
